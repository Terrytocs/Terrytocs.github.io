Home of Theo Scott-Henry

Welcome, wary traveler, to the home of Theodore (Theo/ Ted) G. H. Scott-Henry! You may rest here, before continuing your journey. While you wait, or await your fate, let me tell you a little about myself. I was born in 1991, yes, the same year as Street Fighter II, I am male (he/ him/ his/ they/ them/ their), and live in Mandeville, Jamaica (for now). I like to think I am humourous (I hope I am!), and jovial, but also very driven, and productive (most of the time). I enjoy playing video games, web-development, making game systems (not actual games!), and web-surfing. This site serves as a personal hub to link to various projects that I have worked on, will work on, or will be working on. Feel free to peruse to you heart's desire.

Well, there's nothing here yet, because I recently decided to revamp my profile, so I guess you can check back later, for now.
